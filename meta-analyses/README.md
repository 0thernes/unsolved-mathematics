# 📄 Meta-Analyses

Curated, publishable-format **AI-assisted meta-analyses** of the open problems in
the atlas — one `<slug>.md` per problem. Each synthesizes its
[dossier](../problems/) into a survey: statement and significance, state of the
art, approaches and barriers, a critical assessment, open directions, and selected
references.

> **What these are.** AI-assisted *surveys/assessments*, curated and accredited by
> **Alexander Donahue** (human-in-the-loop). They are **AI-generated and not
> peer-reviewed**. They make **no** claim of new theorems or solutions. Each
> carries a four-model AI review panel (Grok · Gemini · GPT · Claude) and an
> explicit path to human academic verification.

## Integrity
Every file here is checked in CI by `scripts/check_papers.py`: required honesty
banner, author/accreditation, review block, and a scan that rejects any document
claiming its problem is solved. See [SOURCING.md](../docs/methodology/SOURCING.md).
