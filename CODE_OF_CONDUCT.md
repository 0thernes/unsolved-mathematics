# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project pursues mathematical truth in the open. Two commitments bind every
contributor:

1. **Rigor.** Claims are sourced; uncertainty is labeled; nothing is overstated.
   The integrity of the atlas matters more than the speed of any contribution.
2. **Respect.** We critique ideas, never people. Disagreement is expressed
   through evidence and reproducible argument, not derision.

## Expected behavior
- Be precise, be honest, cite your sources.
- Welcome questions and newcomers; mathematics is hard and everyone was new once.
- Assume good faith; correct errors kindly.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Presenting fabricated or unverified citations as established fact.
- Harassment, demeaning comments, or personal attacks.
- Sustained disruption of discussion or review.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and comments that violate
these standards, and may block repeat offenders. Report concerns by opening a
confidential issue or contacting the maintainer.
