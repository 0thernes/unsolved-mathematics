# Editorial Audit Review — "Adversarial Multi-Model Mathematics on an Open Problem" (v3)

**Role:** internal referee report at external-review standard, adversarial posture. **Reviewer:** Claude Fable 5 (a system that co-produced parts of the artifact; declared conflict, mitigated by primary-source grounding of every checked claim). **Date:** 2026-07-01. **Recommendation: accept as a case study, with claims as scoped in v3.** Certification of what that recommendation is *not*: not peer review, not a novelty certificate, not endorsement of any mathematical advance on the conjecture (none is claimed).

## 1. Summary of the submission

The paper documents an eight-hour multi-session AI episode on the Collatz Conjecture: verified frontier facts; four theorems with elementary complete proofs plus one twice-refereed lemma (situated as substantial rediscovery); measured statistics against the Lagarias–Weiss model; a catalogue of ~35 failed resolution claims in four formalized fallacy classes; a verification protocol; a falsifiable priority claim; a proposed benchmark (EDAP); and a negative AGI/ASI assessment.

## 2. Major review findings and their dispositions

**M1 — Novelty overclaim risk (resolved by scoping).** Early drafts gestured at "never happened before." Prior-art search (conducted before v3) surfaced: BrokenMath [17] (documents the fabrication phenomenon), the Adversarial Prover [18] (designed prover/verifier ecologies), SciIntBench [19] (adversarial-framing integrity), Tao's program and AlphaProof [20,21] (verification-as-guardrail consensus), and prior LLM-Collatz work [22]. v3 accordingly claims priority only for the P1–P6 conjunction (§8a) and names its falsification condition. *Reviewer verdict: the scoped claim is defensible; the unscoped one would have been rejected.*

**M2 — AGI/ASI framing (resolved by inversion into a benchmark).** A claim that the episode evidences AGI/ASI would contradict the paper's own §6: the same model families produced Löbian pseudo-proofs under pressure. v3's §8b instead *operationalizes* the episode as an evaluation construct (EDAP) with five metrics and a strict interpretive scope (necessary-dimension, never sufficient). *Verdict: this is the strongest true statement available; any stronger one is self-refuting.*

**M3 — Attribution and logging (flagged, mitigated, not resolvable post hoc).** Model-to-artifact attribution is partly operator-attested; sessions were not cryptographically logged. Mitigation: all *mathematical* claims are grounded in artifacts + primary sources whose validity is attribution-independent. Residual risk: the sociological narrative (which cluster did what) carries attestation-level, not log-level, evidence. Correctly disclosed in §9.

**M4 — Dependency on unaudited material (flagged, quantified).** 179/189 ledger claims remain unaudited; the paper's own tables rely only on the audited 10, the instruments re-run in-session, and primary sources — verified by this review against the audit register. Statements sourced to unaudited documents are either excluded or attributed as targets, not results. *Verdict: clean, provided future edits preserve the boundary.*

**M5 — Single-source computational floor (flagged; field-standard).** The $2^{71}$ verification [1] lacks independent replication; every downstream unconditional bound inherits this trust. The paper discloses it twice. This matches how the field itself cites [1]; no stronger treatment is available to any author.

**M6 — Mathematical checking (performed).** Theorems 3.1–3.4, Lemma 4.1, Corollaries 4.2–4.3, Theorem 5.1's entropy computation ($1-H(\theta)=D(\theta\|\tfrac12)=0.0500444728$), and Theorem 5.2's ladder logic were re-derived during the session by at least one instance independent of the drafting pass; Cor 4.2's original measure-zero gap ($x=-\tfrac13\mapsto0$) was found and repaired *before* publication, and the repair is in the artifact trail. Numerical claims spot-checked this review: $2^{71}=4\cdot2^{69}$; $q_{22}=65{,}470{,}613{,}321$; $S(128)/2^{128}\approx2^{-13.81}$; the descent-law deviation $2.2\times10^{-5}$. No errors found in v3 as published.

**M7 — Figures and data provenance (verified).** Figures 1–2 are generated by a committed, dependency-free script from (a) an exact DP recomputed at build time and (b) tabulated measured records reproducible from `stochastic_model_check.py`. No hand-drawn data.

**M8 — Style and register (minor).** Verdict-level language ("kill-shot", "receipts") was removed from the paper body in v2–v3; remaining prose meets professional register. The operator-background note is phrased as attestation with its dual reading (accessibility and risk) — appropriate.

## 6. Verdict

As a *mathematical* contribution: modest by design — verified frontier synthesis, elementary theorems, one rediscovered-with-margins lemma, honestly labeled. As a *case study in AI epistemics*: the claims are scoped, falsifiable, evidence-linked, and survive this review. **Accept**, with the standing condition that any future edit preserving the audited/unaudited boundary (M4) and the §8 interpretive scope (M2) is required for the acceptance to remain valid.
