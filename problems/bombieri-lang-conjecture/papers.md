### Notes

- The conjecture's defining sources are partly **unpublished**: Bombieri's 1980 formulation circulated through his Chicago seminar and was disseminated in Lang's writings rather than a paper of Bombieri's own. Rows 2, 3, 5, 6, 11 represent Lang's published transmission of the conjectural framework.
- DOIs/arXiv ids are given **only** where confidently recalled. The Caporaso–Harris–Mazur DOI (row 15) is the *J. Amer. Math. Soc.* article; the Lawrence–Venkatesh DOI (row 24) is the *Inventiones* paper "The shape of the Shafarevich/period map proof of Mordell" — the exact title wording is approximate, hence **needs-verification**. Where I am not confident of the precise title or identifier I have written "—" and flagged the row rather than fabricate a number.
- Several entries (Vojta 1991/1996, McQuillan 1995/1998, Campana, Demailly, Brotbek) are real, canonical works in the field, but their exact titles, venues, and identifiers should be checked against MathSciNet/zbMATH before citation; they are marked **needs-verification** accordingly.
