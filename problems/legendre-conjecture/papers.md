**Notes on verification.** Items 1–5, 7–8, 17, and 20–22 are canonical, certainly-real works (the founding statement, the proofs underlying PNT and Bertrand's postulate, Landau's problem list, Hoheisel's and Cramér's gap theorems, the Baker–Harman–Pintz record, and the GPY/Zhang/Maynard small-gap breakthroughs) and are flagged high-confidence. Many entries are marked needs-verification: the authors and the existence of a closely related paper are real and well documented, but I am not fully certain of the exact title wording, the precise year, or the identifier string, and these should be confirmed against a bibliographic source (MathSciNet/zbMATH) before citing. In particular, the exact titles and years of the several "difference between consecutive primes" papers (entries 9, 10, 13, 14, 16) are easily conflated — multiple authors reused near-identical titles across decades — so verify the specific reference you intend to cite. No identifier in this table was fabricated to fill a blank; every "—" marks a real work whose identifier I did not wish to guess.
