> **Atlas Rank #9** · CSS **75.267** · Field: *Mathematical Physics* · Posed: **2000** · Status: `open`  · 🏛️ **Millennium Prize Problem**

## Plain statement

Prove that a quantum Yang–Mills theory exists on R^4 for any compact simple gauge group and has a positive mass gap.
