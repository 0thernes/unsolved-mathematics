## Note on identifiers and verification

Most works on this problem predate arXiv/DOI indexing, and the canonical references (Ramanujan 1916, Mordell 1917, Lehmer 1947, Serre's congruence papers, Deligne 1974, the Edixhoven–Couveignes volume, the Sato–Tate paper) are unambiguously real and easily located by title and author in MathSciNet/zbMATH; these are flagged **high-confidence** with identifier "—" rather than risk a fabricated number. Entries flagged **needs-verification** are real bodies of work whose *exact title, year, or venue* as listed here should be confirmed before citation (in particular #5, #8, #13, #14, #15, #18, #19, #24, #25 — the Murty–Murty–Shorey effective-value result and the Bennett et al. value-equation papers are genuine but their precise bibliographic data and the single arXiv id shown should be checked). Entries flagged **ai-suggested** (#16, #17, #22, #23) stand in for genuine survey/computational literature whose specific canonical reference I cannot pin down with confidence; they should be replaced with verified citations. No identifier has been invented; every "—" denotes a real work whose stable identifier was not asserted rather than a placeholder for a missing paper.
