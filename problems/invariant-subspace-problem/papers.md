Exact titles, years, and authorship for several survey/expository entries (#6, #10, #16, #18, #20, #22, #24) are reconstructed from memory and may contain inaccuracies in title wording, year, or attribution; #18 is flagged *ai-suggested* because the precise authorship of short "simplified Read" expositions is uncertain. No identifier has been invented; every "—" reflects a deliberate omission rather than a fabricated number. The 2023 Enflo preprint (#25) is listed as a notable development, not as an accepted resolution — see status.md.
