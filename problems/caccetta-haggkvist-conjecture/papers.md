**Note on shortfall and reliability.** The Caccetta–Häggkvist literature is real and substantial, but it is dispersed across conference proceedings, journals, and arXiv preprints whose exact titles, years, and identifiers I cannot fully verify from memory. The genuinely canonical works are rows 2 (the originating paper, Caccetta–Häggkvist 1978), 4 (Chvátal–Szemerédi's additive-constant theorem), 10 (Shen Jian's $0.3532\,n$ triangle bound, 2003), 13 (Razborov's flag algebras, the method behind the best bound), 15 (Chudnovsky–Seymour–Sullivan, *Cycles in dense digraphs*), and 19 (Hladký–Král'–Norin, the $\approx 0.3465\,n$ bound). Several rows are flagged **ai-suggested** because, while works of that kind plausibly exist, I cannot confirm a specific paper, author, or identifier; these should be treated as leads to verify, not as established citations. The Razborov DOI (row 13) and the two arXiv ids (rows 18, 19) are given as **needs-verification** because I am not fully certain they are exact. No identifier here should be cited without independent confirmation.
