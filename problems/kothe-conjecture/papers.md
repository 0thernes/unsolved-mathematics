**Note on identifiers and shortfall.** This subject's canonical literature is overwhelmingly pre-1995 and pre-arXiv (Köthe 1930 in *Math. Z.*; Levitzki, Amitsur, Krempa, Golod–Shafarevich in journals not indexed by DOI at the time), so reliable persistent identifiers are largely unavailable; rather than fabricate DOIs/arXiv numbers (prohibited), all rows show "—". Rows 1–3, 5, 7, 9, 13, 15–17 are canonical, certainly-real works and are flagged high-confidence. Several later rows (titles/years approximate from memory of the Puczyłowski–Smoktunowicz body of work on the Köthe problem) are flagged needs-verification: the *authors and topic* are reliable but the exact title or year should be checked against MathSciNet/zbMATH before citation. The genuinely indispensable, verifiable core is Köthe (1930), Amitsur (radicals of polynomial rings), Krempa (1972 reductions), Golod–Shafarevich (1964), and Smoktunowicz (2000–2002).
