Items flagged "needs-verification" are real strands of work whose exact title, venue, year, or numbering I could not reproduce with confidence, so they carry an em-dash rather than an invented identifier. Confident identifiers are given only for Vojta's LNM 1239 (#12) and Elkies's lattice-reduction paper (#18, *Algorithmic Number Theory* / arXiv math/0005139). No identifier has been fabricated.
