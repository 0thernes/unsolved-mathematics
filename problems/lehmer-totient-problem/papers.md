Several rows (notably #3, #11, #17, #21–#25) are plausible but uncertain in their exact title, year, or attribution and are flagged accordingly; some may conflate or mis-date real works. Identifiers have been deliberately withheld ("—") wherever I cannot assert a DOI/arXiv id with confidence, in preference to fabricating one.
