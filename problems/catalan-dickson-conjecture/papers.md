**Note on completeness and sourcing.** Aliquot-sequence research is concentrated in a handful of canonical references (Catalan's 1888 note; Dickson 1913 and his *History*; Guy–Selfridge 1975; Guy's *Unsolved Problems in Number Theory* §B6; the Bosma–Kane "aliquot constant"; and the Pomerance–Pollack–Kobayashi structural papers), supplemented by a large body of *computational* output (te Riele, Creyaufmüller, Zimmermann, FactorDB) that is often distributed as web tables and project notes rather than formally published articles with DOIs. Several rows above describe real lines of work whose exact titles, years, or venues I cannot pin down with confidence; these are flagged "needs-verification" and carry "—" rather than a fabricated identifier. The DOI given for row 15 is the Springer book series identifier for *Unsolved Problems in Number Theory* and should be confirmed against the specific edition. No arXiv ids are asserted because I am not confident of exact arXiv numbers for these works; readers should verify via MathSciNet/zbMATH and the OEIS cross-references (e.g. A098007, A003023, A005114 for untouchable numbers).
